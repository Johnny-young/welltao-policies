WellTao Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 23, 2025
Last Updated: November 13, 2025

Welcome to WellTao (“the App”, “we”, or “us”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, share, and safeguard your information when you use WellTao.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information necessary to operate and improve our services. We do not collect any data unrelated to the App’s functionality.

1.1 Login & Account Information (User-Provided)

When you use Sign in with Apple, we receive:

  • Apple’s unique userIdentifier
  • Authentication credentials (identityToken, authorizationCode, nonce)
  • Email, nickname, or name (only if provided by Apple during first authorization)

This information is used only to create or log in to your WellTao account.

1.2 Body Constitution Test Data

  • Age (range)
  • Gender
  • Questionnaire responses
  • Wellness preferences

1.3 AI Interaction Data

Your messages to the AI, and the AI-generated responses, are sent to an external model provider (currently DeepSeek) strictly to generate responses.

1.4 Subscription Data (via RevenueCat)

  • Your WellTao user ID
  • Subscription tier
  • Start and expiration dates
  • Grace period / retry status
  • Product ID

1.5 Data Automatically Collected by RevenueCat

  • Device model
  • OS version
  • App version
  • Region/language (not precise location)
  • Anonymous device identifier

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

1.6 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • IP address
  • Precise or coarse location
  • IDFA or advertising tracking identifiers
  • Contacts, camera, microphone, photos
  • Any iOS system permissions

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide login and account services
  • Generate constitution analysis and recommendations
  • Send text to AI models for responses
  • Manage subscription access
  • Support anonymous mode for non-logged-in users
  • Display past test history

We do not use your data for profiling, advertising, or cross-app tracking.

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Essential Service Providers

  • RevenueCat — subscription processing
  • AI model provider — text input only, for generating responses

3.2 Legal Obligations

We may disclose your data if required to comply with applicable laws or government requests.

3.3 What We Never Do

  • No selling of personal data
  • No sharing of health data with advertisers
  • No sharing of data unrelated to core service functions

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on DigitalOcean (USA) servers.

4.2 Retention Period

Your data is stored as long as your account remains active. Backups may temporarily retain deleted data but are never used operationally.

4.3 Security Measures

  • HTTPS / TLS encryption
  • Server access control
  • Authentication and security logs
  • Access limited to authorized developers

5. Your Rights

  • Access subscription info inside the App
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account via the App or by email
  • Withdraw Apple login usage
  • Contact us for any privacy concerns

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred and processed outside your country of residence, specifically in the United States, where our servers are hosted.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Important changes will be communicated via in-app notifications or email.
