WellTao – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 23, 2025
Last Updated: November 13, 2025

WellTao (“we”, “our”, “the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Sign in with Apple (User-Provided)

When you log in using Sign in with Apple, we receive:

  • Apple userIdentifier
  • identityToken, authorizationCode, nonce
  • Email or name (only if Apple allows sharing)

This data is used only to create and log in to your WellTao account.

1.2 Constitution Test Data

  • Age (range or category)
  • Gender
  • Answers to constitution questionnaire
  • Lifestyle preferences

Used to generate your constitution result and wellness recommendations.

1.3 AI Interaction Data

  • Your messages/questions sent to AI
  • Generated responses (stored as history)

Your text is sent to external AI service providers (DeepSeek, or similar). It is not used to identify you or train global models.

1.4 Subscription Data (via RevenueCat)

We receive and store:

  • Your WellTao user ID
  • Subscription type and status
  • Start / expiration date
  • Grace period or billing retry state
  • Product ID

1.5 RevenueCat SDK (Automatically Collected by RevenueCat)

  • Device model
  • OS version
  • App version
  • Language/region setting
  • Anonymized RC device identifiers

Used for subscription validation and fraud prevention.

1.6 What We Do NOT Collect

  • No IP address
  • No location data
  • No device fingerprint
  • No photos, contacts, microphone, camera
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • No system permissions

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Account creation & login
  • Generate constitution assessments & wellness suggestions
  • Submit your text to AI for responses
  • Subscription management
  • Display your history and personalized features

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

3. Sharing of Information

3.1 Necessary Service Providers

  • RevenueCat – subscription services
  • AI API providers – to generate responses from your text

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose data only if required by law.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on DigitalOcean servers located in the United States.

Security measures include HTTPS/TLS encryption, access control, and limited developer access.

Some data may remain temporarily in encrypted backups.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your subscription status
  • Request correction
  • Request account deletion (in-app or email)
  • Withdraw consent by stopping service use

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be announced via in-app notifications or email.
