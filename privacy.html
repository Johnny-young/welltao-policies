WellTao Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 23, 2025
Last Updated: November 13, 2025

Welcome to WellTao (“the App”, “we”, or “us”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, share, and safeguard your information when you use WellTao.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information necessary to operate and improve our services. We do not collect any data unrelated to the App’s functionality.

1.1 Login & Account Information (User-Provided)

When you use Sign in with Apple, we receive:

  • Apple’s unique userIdentifier
  • Authentication credentials (identityToken, authorizationCode, nonce)
  • Email, nickname, or name (only if provided by Apple during first authorization)

This information is used only to create or log in to your WellTao account.

1.2 Body Constitution Test Data

  • Age (range)
  • Gender
  • Questionnaire responses
  • Wellness preferences

1.3 AI Interaction Data

Your messages to the AI, and the AI-generated responses, are sent to an external model provider (currently DeepSeek) strictly to generate responses.

1.4 Subscription Data (via RevenueCat)

  • Your WellTao user ID
  • Subscription tier
  • Start and expiration dates
  • Grace period / retry status
  • Product ID

1.5 Data Automatically Collected by RevenueCat

  • Device model
  • OS version
  • App version
  • Region/language (not precise location)
  • Anonymous device identifier
